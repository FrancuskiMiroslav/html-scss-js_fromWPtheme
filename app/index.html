Plant-Based Gelato

At Gigi, we Transform pure and Fresh
Fruit & Veggie Cold-pressed Juices
into Italian Gelato.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create deliciousness out of plant based ingredients.
We take natural raw materials, turning them into top quality
products that improve people’s happiness.

Our mission

At Gigi , We Transform pure Fruit & Vegetable Cold-pressed Juices into Italian Gelato.

As lovers of great taste, we developed Gigi as a healthier ice cream solution but without
avoiding the pleasure of the Gelato experience. Therefore, Gigi ’s unique flavors
combinations are creamy as a traditional gelato but
without the cream and the guilt.

Our Vision

Our Italian artisan gelato maestro and professor at the ice cream school in Rome, “Gigi” , has been
working in the ice cream field for more than twenty years, and is known for its unique product
formulation, its profound knowledge of raw materials and sophisticated technologies.

We developed a unique technology that al lows us to substitute animal fat,
such as milk/cream/eggs, with a composition of different plantbased fibers,
hereby leaving out any chemical or animal-based additives and avoid any
hot processing during the production in order to preserve the
nutrients of the natural ingredients used.
